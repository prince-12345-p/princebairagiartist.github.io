About Prince Bairagi

Biography

Born into a musical family, Prince Bairagi began his piano studies at the age of five. His exceptional talent was quickly recognized, leading to his first public performance at age seven. He studied at the prestigious Juilliard School under the tutelage of Maestro Richard Kinley, graduating with highest honors.

Prince's performances are known for their emotional depth and technical precision. He has developed a unique voice that honors classical traditions while embracing contemporary innovations. His 2022 recording of Beethoven's sonatas received the Gramophone Editor's Choice award.

When not performing, Prince dedicates time to composing original works and mentoring young musicians through his foundation. He believes music is the universal language that can bridge cultures and generations.
